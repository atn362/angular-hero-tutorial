    Imagine Jane and John are hiking on a trail. At every mile on this trail there
        as mile marker. Jane is standing at the third mile marker and John is standing
        at the eighth. The user needs to know how many inches Jane is from John.

Requirements:
        1. The program must calculate and store the miles, feet, and inches
            for the distance between Jane and John
        2. The program must print the message...
                "Jane is [x] inches from John!"
                 (replace [x] with the calculated inches value)

Hints:
        - A mile is 5280 feet
        - A foot is 12 inches.
